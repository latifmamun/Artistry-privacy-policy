Privacy Policy

Artistry – Digital Drawing & Painting App

Last updated: December 09, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Artistry! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal data. Artistry is designed to work completely offline and locally on your device.

  • Drawing files (.png) are saved only in your device's internal storage or gallery (you choose).
  • No account, login, or email is required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools are used.
  • No data is ever sent to any server.

3. Permissions Explained

The app requests the following permissions only when you use specific features:

  • Storage / Photos & Media – Required to save your artwork to Gallery or load images from your device.
  • Read/Write External Storage (Android 12 and below) – Same purpose as above.

You can deny these permissions — the app will still work for drawing, but saving/loading from gallery will be disabled.

4. No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit any user data to third parties because no data is collected.

5. Children's Privacy

Artistry is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we fully comply with COPPA and GDPR-K.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this page periodically.
